Who is Cash Compton?

Updated December 2022

I have survived 28 winters, have a degree in Computer Science Engineering, and live in my self-converted camper van with my dog, Quinn. I camp primarily on small farms where I volunteer and in return get to live sustainably, in community, surrounded by amazing people, plants, and animals who teach me the most important lessons of life. My goal is to create harmony between humans and nature.
